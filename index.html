About

Hello! I am a graduate student in the Language Technologies Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am advised by Graham Neubig in the NeuLab. I am supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

Prior to CMU, I received my Bachelor's degree at UC Irvine (zot zot!), where I was a member of the Computation of Language Laboratory (CoLaLab).

Research

Currently, I am interested in machine translation, a subfield of natural language processing (NLP). Among other things, I am developing assistive technology for simultaneous interpreters, which entails designing systems that process language incrementally.
